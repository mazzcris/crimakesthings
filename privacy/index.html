Privacy

This is a personal maker portfolio. It keeps your privacy simple: there are no cookies, nothing is stored on your device, and you are not tracked across sites or profiled.

To get a rough idea of how many people visit, the site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a cookieless, privacy-friendly tool. It collects only anonymous, aggregate statistics — things like page views, referrers and approximate country — and does not set cookies, store your IP address, sell data, or identify you personally.

Outbound links (Instagram, the marketplaces where designs are published, etc.) take you to third-party sites with their own privacy policies.
